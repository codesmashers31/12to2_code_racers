Selling Tips

Take Great Photos

Use good lighting and take clear photos from multiple angles. Highlight any special features or flaws.

Write a Detailed Description

Be honest about the condition and include all relevant details like size, components, and maintenance history.

Price Fairly

Research similar cycles to set a competitive price. Consider the condition, age, and original price.

Be Responsive

Respond promptly to inquiries and be available to answer questions from potential buyers.
